NIKE - How it Started

The company was founded on January 25, 1964, as "Blue Ribbon Sports", by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, and officially became Nike, Inc. on May 30, 1971.

1964

Nike First 'Moon' Shoe

Nike was established as Blue Ribbon Sports, introducing its first shoe, the Nike "Moon Shoe," marking the initial step toward becoming a global powerhouse in athletic footwear and apparel.

CONTINUE READING

1972

Collab with Jordan.

Nike collaborated with Michael Jordan, releasing the iconic Air Jordan line of basketball shoes, forever changing the sneaker industry and solidifying Nike's position as a leader in athletic footwear.

1985

Air Max 1(revolutionary cusion tech)

Nike introduced the Air Max 1, a revolutionary shoe featuring visible Air cushioning technology. This innovation set the stage for Nike's ongoing commitment to comfort, performance, and iconic sneaker designs.

1987

NFL's official sportwear provider

Nike became the official uniform and apparel provider for the National Football League (NFL), solidifying its presence in professional sports and expanding its brand visibility worldwide.
